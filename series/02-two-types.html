Two Types of Cooperation, Two Types of Crisis

The c₁ parameter and universality classes

Not all prisoner's dilemmas are equal. The difference between international trade and nuclear disarmament is captured in a single number: c₁ = T + S − R − P.

When c₁ > 0, temptation dominates. The system has a unique equilibrium, smooth transitions, predictable dynamics. This is the world of classical economics.

When c₁ < 0, vulnerability dominates. Multiple equilibria, hysteresis, path dependence. History matters. Recovering cooperation requires improving institutions far more than what degraded them to lose it.
