The Empirical Evidence

Do the model's predictions work?

An elegant theory without evidence is speculation. What predictions does the TWC framework make?

Prediction 1: Computational capacity predicts output. Using Penn World Table data, we find R² = 95%.

Prediction 2: Patents measure the advance of Ωₙ. USPTO data confirms >90% correlation.

Prediction 3: The model predicts out of sample. Theil's U = 0.24, significantly better than naive forecasting.

These are not definitive proofs—correlations can have spurious causes. But they are consistent with theoretical predictions and show the framework captures something real about economic structure.
